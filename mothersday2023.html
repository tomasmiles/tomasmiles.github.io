Happy Mother's Day

To Mum

Once I was a little shoot, barely there,

Hiding.

You shared your food, I grew, I kicked.

Eventually ejected into the world outside you.

Once I could not hold my own head high.

Screaming wordlessly. Guess my needs!

Who knew there was a time and place to potty?

For the longest time, not I.

The years under the same roof, still I am fed.

The old challenges surpassed, new challenges arise.

Eager to grow, I did not know

How lucky I was to have all I did.

You taught me what it means to be a good parent.

If I'm lucky I'll be half as good as you.

Thank you Mum.

Lots of love,

Tomas
